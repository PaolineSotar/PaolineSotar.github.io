HISTOIRES GRISES -

Apports du design graphique à un imaginaire collectif confisqué.

Thesis for my master degree in graphic design at Les Arènes in Toulouse. The research was based on the context of the Argentinian dictatorship during the 70’. More specifically on the next generations’ amnesia due to the lack on info and the amount of mysteries that still persist on this time. The aim wasn’t to re-write the story but to confront to small stories, to take an interest either on average Argentinian citizen or specialists (historian, sociologist, psychologist…). Focused on graphic design, I wonder about the ability of this one to unite and propose spaces of meeting between different generations of Argentinians. The research was also based on the ghost-image, the missing-image, and the human’s need to balance the void. This thesis came with a graphic design project, available here, that respond to all the questions I raise. The document in .pdf is available on demand.
