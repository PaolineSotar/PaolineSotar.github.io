FERIA MIGRA - Buenos Aires Art Book Fair

MIGRA is an Independent Collective based in Buenos Aires that arises from the need to generate a space in community for containment, support and growth that allows to represent and make visible emerging and established projects within the scope of publications and visual arts. MIGRA ART BOOK FAIR is the Buenos Aires Art Book Fair and a collective of publications and printed art matter. The objective of the fair is to encourage and be a space for exchange, production and representation from a educational, aesthetic and contemporary perspective to the printed art matter community. At the same time it is a thematic fair of open call where different activities related to printed art are made from talks, readings, projections, presentations and small self-management workshops in which artists and editorials participate from different cities and countries of the world. Work in collaboration with the graphic design studio Lamasburgariotti during my internship in 2018.

L'objectif de la foire est d'encourager et d'être un espace d'échange, de production et de représentation d'un point de vue éducatif, esthétique et contemporain à l'art imprimé.
